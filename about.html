👤 Who I Am

Ololade Olaoluwa Suberu — cybersecurity student, tech enthusiast, and aspiring security professional. Born on March 17, 2007, I've been fascinated by technology for as long as I can remember. That curiosity evolved into a clear purpose: to protect people in the digital world.

I'm currently pursuing my studies in Information Technology and Cybersecurity at Moreno Valley College in California, with an expected graduation of Spring 2027. From there, I plan to transfer to California State University, San Bernardino to earn my Bachelor's degree — and ultimately my Master's — deepening my expertise in IT security and infrastructure.

My goal isn't just to build a career — it's to build a safer internet. I want to eliminate the fear and risk that prevent people from trusting and fully utilizing the digital world. Whether through hands-on security work, building tools, or educating others, I'm committed to making cybersecurity accessible and actionable for everyone.

🎯 Mission

OloladeSec exists to make cybersecurity accessible, practical, and proactive. In a world where digital threats evolve daily, the mission is clear: equip individuals and organizations with the tools, knowledge, and strategies they need to stay protected.

Every tool built, every analysis shared, and every piece of knowledge created reflects a commitment to precision, transparency, and real-world impact. Security isn't just about defense — it's about building trust in a digital-first world and empowering people to navigate the internet without fear.
